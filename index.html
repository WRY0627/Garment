Nerfies: Deformable Neural Radiance Fields

Keunhong Park1, Utkarsh Sinha2, Jonathan T. Barron2, Sofien Bouaziz2, Dan B Goldman2, Steven M. Seitz1,2, Ricardo Martin-Brualla2
1University of Washington, 2Google Research

Nerfies turns selfie videos from your phone into free-viewpoint portraits.

Abstract

We present Nerfies, a method for creating free-viewpoint portraits from casually captured videos.

Our approach leverages neural radiance fields (NeRFs) and a deformation model to reconstruct dynamic scenes with high fidelity.

We demonstrate applications of our technique for novel view synthesis, animation, and re-rendering.
