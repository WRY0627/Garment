SemanticGarment

Pipeline

Given a text or image prompt, our method leverages the semantic clothing model to categorize and initialize point clouds for different clothing types. We design an interpolated densification strategy to increase point density and initialize the garment Gaussians. Dual-modal pretrained 2D diffusion models are employed to refine geometric and texture details, resulting in the final 3D Gaussians. Semantic editing and self-occlusion optimization enhance the generated garments to better meet user requirements. This framework enables the efficient generation of high-fidelity, controllable, and body-structure-compliant garments within a short timeframe.
